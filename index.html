I was born on a pirate ship, with apples all around me. No one ever told me who my parents were. I was raised on apple butter, apple pie, apple crisp, apple cobbler, apple juice, and apples. I always wondered why the pirates didn't raid other ships of a different fruit.

When I finally found land at the age of 19, my first friend was a dog named Pubcrawl. Pubcrawl and I often spent weeks apart, but we'd always reunite on the 15th of each month to swap stories and share some sausages she stealthily stole acquired at the Monday market.

After 12 years of this lifestyle, Pubcrawl and I parted ways. She was going back to sea for another many moons, she said. I vowed to never spend another night on open waters, so we had to say goodbye. Hope to see you soon, Pubcrawl...
